What is Beach Glass Boutique?

Meet the CEO

Hi! My name is Cori and I created Beach Glass Boutique. I grew up with the beach minutes away from my house so it is a second home to me. I wanted to start a place where all my favorite beach clothes and supplies were all in once location. Beach Glass is my passion and a place I want everyone to walk into and be ready to run to the sand. The name was curated by the love my family and I have of walking down the shore looking for beach glass. We could spend hours searching and the beach itself has created so many memories, I hope that people also experience in their life.
